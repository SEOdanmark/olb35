Does PRP reverse aging?

Is PRP really worth it?

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) therapy has shown promising results in various medical and cosmetic applications. Its effectiveness depends on individual factors and the specific condition being treated. Is PRP once a year enough?

The frequency of PRP treatments depends on various factors, including the individual's response to the therapy, the specific condition being treated, and the desired outcome. In some cases, annual treatments may be sufficient to maintain results, while others may require more frequent sessions.

Does PRP regrow hair permanently?

PRP has shown promising results in promoting hair growth for some individuals with certain types of hair loss. However, the effects may not be permanent, and maintenance treatments may be necessary to sustain the results.
